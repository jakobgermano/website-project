The Great Blue Whale

Did you know that the blue whale is the biggest mammal on earth? Weighing in at about 200 tons as a full grown adult! and can grown to around 100 feet long! The reason we call them blue whales is because naturally they are colored grey but when they dive deep into the ocean because of the water and the sunlight they appear to be a darker blue. They are also known as the loudest animal on the planet and live to about 90 years old!


The Diet

The blue whale doesnt eat a wide veriaty of food, but when its hungry it eats these guys through what they call filter feeding

  • Krill
  • Fish
  • Copepods

A copepod is a tiny crustacean just like crabs or lobsters

Fun fact! They actually eat around 12,000lbs of krill a day

Behavior

  • Blue whales typically swim alone or in small pairs
  • They communicate using low frequency pulses, groans, and moans
  • There has been research that blue whales feel emotion

Blue whales are actually in every ocean expect the Artic!
